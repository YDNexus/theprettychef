Meet the Chef

Beauty, Brains &
Bold Flavors

Born and raised in Atlanta, Chef Essence Alina Ray is the definition of a Bad Bitch who can throw down in the kitchen. With grace, style, and a whole lot of flavor, she's redefining what it means to eat plant-based.

Fluent in both vegan and vegetarian cuisine, Essence brings an artistry to every plate that's as stunning as she is. Her philosophy? Food should be gorgeous, nourishing, and absolutely unforgettable — just like the Princess who made it.

From intimate dinner parties in Buckhead to large-scale events in Midtown, Chef Essence has built a reputation as Atlanta's most sought-after plant-based private chef. She doesn't follow trends — she sets them.
